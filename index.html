The Fundamental Story

Calloused Hands. Northern Italian Heart.

"If you're going to take the risk to change your career, invest your retirement savings, and work 7 days a week on Main Street—it better be for something authentic."

For over 30 years, Bob Hoxsie swung hammers across Southern California construction sites running Hoxsie Builders Inc. He knew what it took to build structures that endure: uncompromising craftsmanship, premium materials, and honest work. But his heritage always pulled him back to his family roots in Northern Italy.

During family pilgrimages to Italy, Bob and his wife Tanya Hoxsie (powerhouse CEO of the Boys & Girls Clubs of Huntington Valley) fell in love with traditional gelateria culture. When they tasted real, daily-churned gelato, they realized a heartbreaking truth: most dessert sold in America is fake. It's warehouse-manufactured, chemically stabilized, and shipped solid across state lines.

In 2009, they opened Mangiamo at 122 Main Street. In 2015, Bob was honored as Huntington Beach’s Outstanding Citizen of the Year. Today, every single cup is a small act of defiance against mass-produced commercial ice cream.
