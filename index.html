The Spatula Tradition

We never use mechanical spring-loaded ice cream scoops. Authentic Northern Italian gelato is sculpted and served exclusively with a flat stainless steel spatula (la spatola) to preserve its dense, velvety emulsion.

The Family Legacy

From SoCal Construction Hardhats to Northern Italian Gelatieri

For over 30 years, Bob Hoxsie worked across Southern California construction sites running Hoxsie Builders Inc. He knew what it took to build structures that endure: uncompromising standards, premium raw materials, and honest sweat. But his heritage always pulled him back to his family roots in Northern Italy.

During family pilgrimages to Italy, Bob and his wife Tanya Hoxsie (powerhouse CEO of the Boys & Girls Clubs of Huntington Valley) fell in love with traditional gelateria culture. When they tasted real, daily-churned artisan gelato, they realized a heartbreaking truth: most gelato in the United States is fake. It's warehouse-made, chemically stabilized, and shipped solid.

"If you're going to take the risk to change your career, invest your retirement savings, and work 7 days a week on Main Street—it better be for something extraordinary."

In 2009, they opened Mangiamo Gelato Caffe at 122 Main Street. In 2015, Bob was named Huntington Beach’s Outstanding Citizen of the Year. Today, they still operate with a craftsman's ethos: no shortcuts, no factory boxes, pure Northern Italian soul.
